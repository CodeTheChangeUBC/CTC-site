Big Brothers

Data Aggregation

×

Big Brothers

Data Aggregation

We helped Big Brothers optimize their placement of clothing donation bins across the Lower Mainland by aggregating and visualizing information from different sources.

Sixty percent of the funding for Big Brothers Vancouver comes from clothing donations. They sell the donations to Value Village to make a profit and need to place their donation bins strategically across the lower mainland. The goal of this project was to help Big Brothers with their problem by building a web application that will pull donation bin locations from many different sources and put them all on one nice map so they can see where they should place theirs.

Project Lead: Marshall McDougall
Team: Anthony, Daniel, Jon
