Volunteer With Us

We believe that software isn't a way to solve social issues in and of itself. Rather, its power is in supporting organizations who already have the relevant expertise in a given area.

Students form the backbone of our organization. By helping students use their skills to solve current and substantial problems we hope to empower and encourage the next generation of philanthropists. Our goal is to show students that their work can immediately have a significant social impact.

We welcome students with a wide variety of skills and faculties. We graciously accept applications all year round. Twice a year (September and January), we will be starting a new set of projects which are expected to take approximately one school term.

If you would like to participate or if you have any questions at all, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. We look forward to hearing your ideas!
