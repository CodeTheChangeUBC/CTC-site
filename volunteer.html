Volunteer With Us

Why Volunteer?

We believe that software isn't a way to solve social issues in and of itself. Rather, its power is in supporting organizations who already have the relevant expertise in a given area.

Students and graduates form the backbone of our organization. By helping people use their skills to solve current and substantial problems we hope to empower and encourage the next generation of philanthropists. Our goal is to show everyone that their work can immediately have a significant social impact.

We welcome everyione with a wide variety of skills and faculties. If you'd like to apply please browse our current open positions and see if there's something for you.

If you would like to participate or if you have any questions at all, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. We look forward to hearing your ideas!

Hiring Process

Apply
  1. View the open positions and the job description(s) posted.
  2. Have a resume handy.
  3. Apply for the job you want with the form provided.
Review We review all candidates on a rolling basis, which means we review applications and conduct interviews on a first come, first serve basis. Make sure to get your application in early!
Interview

Within a week of submitting your application, you will receive an email asking for an in-person interview. Pick the date and time that works for you! The interview will cover:

  • Personality Questions
  • Personal Projects
  • Technical/Programming Questions
Job Offer

An email will go out to all applicants when the application deadline closes, regardless of the final decision.

  • If you choose to accept your job offer, please confirm your offer and attendance in the first meeting. Congratulations!
  • If a job offer is not extended, we are more than happy to discuss improvements for next time!
First Meeting Welcome to our first meeting! We will be introducing your new team members for the year and projects that we will be working on.
