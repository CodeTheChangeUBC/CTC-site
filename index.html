About Us

Code The Change UBC builds open-source software solutions to enhance the effectiveness of non-profit organizations. We believe in the unification of technology and philanthropy. Our passionate and diverse team proudly enables incredible organizations to acheive their altruistic goals.

Code the Change began in 2009 as a student organizations at Stanford University. Since then, multiple chapters across North America, including CTC UBC, have developed to carry on the mission.
